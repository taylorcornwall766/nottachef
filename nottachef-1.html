ABOUT US / WHO WE ARE

We are a group of people who enjoy cooking, suck at it, and love to eat. For 19 years we have been eating over 5 times a day, not out of necessity, but out of desire. We will never stop eating, no matter how full we get. We love desert, have one with every meal. Eggs? Cake would go nicely after.

OUR MISSION

Our goal is to share that passion of eating and cooking to the everyday joe. We do that by sharing recipes that are both simple and cost effective. No one should be left high and dry, ESPECIALLY those living in food deserts who are restricted to shopping at places like dollar general for the majority of their food products.

OUR PARTNERS
